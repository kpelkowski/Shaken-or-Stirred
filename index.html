At some events, a host may desire to pay for an open bar for their guests for a limited time and then elect to switch it to a cash bar. This allows the host to provide a bar for his guests even after the host (open) bar has ended. We can accommodate this request by utilizing the Sunflower Host Bar option during the time period of the Open bar and then revert to a Cash Bar option. During the Host Bar time frame a tip jar will NOT be displayed, however, an 18% gratuity is added to the host's cost. Once the Cash Bar has started a tip jar will be displayed.

Would you like to provide a non-alcoholic self serve station? We can provide a refreshing iced water and/or iced tea station! We can even infuse water with fruits of your choice. Costs for this beverage station vary in range depending on your guest count. Ask us for a quote!

About

The Shaken or Stirred Bartending business began out of a love for entertaining and mixology. We take pride in seeing the smiles of people who are enjoying the beverages we serve. Allow us to bring our passion to your event. We will work with you and your needs for the affair to make sure that your guests are entertained and happy.

Responsibility

At Shaken or Stirred Bartending we believe that good times and having fun are essential to events and our service can play a part in that. We stand behind responsible drinking and prevention of underage drinking. Our bartenders are instructed not to serve any customer who appears to have had too much to drink already. In addition, our bartenders will check for proper identification to ensure the customer is old enough to drink alchohol in accordance with state law.
